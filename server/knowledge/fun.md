# Personal Interests and Motivation

## What Inspired Me to Work in AI

I became interested in artificial intelligence because it combines several areas I enjoy: mathematics, programming, and systems thinking. What fascinates me most is the ability to build systems that can reason about information, learn from data, and assist humans in solving complex problems.

Rather than treating AI as a standalone model, I enjoy exploring how intelligent systems can be integrated into real software environments such as operating systems, developer tools, and interactive applications.

## What I Enjoy Building

I enjoy building systems where machine learning interacts with larger software architectures. This includes projects where AI models are embedded into developer tools, operating systems, or real-world applications.

Some of my favourite types of projects involve:

- AI-powered developer environments
- Retrieval systems that help users find information quickly
- Computer vision systems that interact with the physical world
- Intelligent assistants built on top of structured knowledge bases

## Problems I Like Solving

I enjoy working on problems that involve both engineering and reasoning. In particular, I like designing systems that combine multiple components such as machine learning models, backend services, and user interfaces.

I am especially interested in challenges related to:

- building reliable AI systems
- improving how humans interact with intelligent software
- designing scalable retrieval and knowledge systems
- integrating AI into everyday development workflows

## What Motivates Me

What motivates me most is the process of turning complex ideas into working systems. I enjoy experimenting with new architectures, building prototypes, and refining them into practical tools.

I find it rewarding when a project moves beyond theory and becomes something people can actually interact with, whether it is an AI assistant, a developer tool, or an intelligent application.
